# Writing Patterns

## Basic information

Use a compact header. Avoid self-evaluation such as “学习能力强、认真负责”.

Recommended fields:
- Name
- Job intention, e.g. `求职意向：产品经理实习生 / 产品经理`
- Phone, email, city
- Portfolio or personal homepage if relevant

Education format:
`学校｜专业｜学历｜时间`
Optional details: relevant courses, GPA/rank, awards, exchange experience. Keep details short.

## Experience entry structure

Use this shape for 工作经历、实习经历、项目经历:

1. Entry heading: `公司/组织｜岗位/项目角色｜时间`
2. One-sentence context: `围绕[业务/用户/场景]，为解决[问题/目标]，参与/负责[模块]，产出[结果/沉淀]。`
3. Bullets: action + method + deliverable + result.

Good bullet formula:
`动词 + 对象/问题 + 方法/工具 + 产出物 + 结果/影响`

Examples:
- `参与会员转化链路优化，基于埋点数据拆解注册-付费漏斗，定位权益说明不清与支付入口层级深的问题，输出原型与需求文档，推动转化率提升约 8%。`
- `协助搭建运营数据看板，梳理核心指标口径并与研发确认埋点方案，沉淀日/周报模板，将人工统计时间从 2 小时缩短至 30 分钟。`

## Project splitting inside one company

If projects are similar, keep one entry with 3-4 bullets.
If projects show different PM capabilities, split under subtitles:

`项目一：用户增长活动`
- bullets

`项目二：后台数据看板`
- bullets

Use at most 2 project subtitles per company unless the user has senior experience and enough page budget.

## Product manager capability vocabulary

Prefer concrete PM work:
- 用户研究: 访谈、问卷、竞品分析、用户分层、场景拆解
- 需求分析: 需求池、优先级、PRD、原型、流程图、状态机、验收标准
- 数据分析: 漏斗、留存、转化、活跃、GMV、CTR、CVR、SQL、埋点
- 项目推进: 排期、评审、跨团队沟通、风险同步、验收、复盘
- 增长/运营: 活动策略、A/B 测试、触达策略、用户生命周期
- AI 产品: 提示词、模型评测、知识库、RAG、Agent 流程、人工审核闭环

Avoid vague words without evidence:
- `深度参与`, `全面负责`, `赋能业务`, `显著提升`, `打造闭环`

## Bullet length

Default target:
- 18-32 Chinese characters for tight bullets.
- 35-55 Chinese characters for important bullets with metrics.
- Avoid multi-clause bullets over 70 Chinese characters unless necessary.
